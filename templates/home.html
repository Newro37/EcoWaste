Waste Classification Guide

Proper classification helps collectors prioritize and respond efficiently.

Organic

Food scraps, yard waste, and biodegradable materials. Composting reduces methane emissions.

Biodegradable

Plastic

Bottles, containers and packaging. Recycling prevents microplastic ocean pollution.

Recyclable

Metal

Cans, foil, and scrap metal. Recycling metals saves energy vs. raw material mining.

Recyclable

E-Waste

Electronics, batteries, and appliances. Prevents heavy metal groundwater contamination.

Special Handling

Hazardous

Chemicals, paint, and medical waste. Requires professional disposal to prevent health risks.
